Meet the Visionaries Behind Microworld Biotech

Founded by two industry veterans with a combined experience of over 45 years in the pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors, Microworld Biotech was built to bridge the gap between laboratory-grade accuracy and real-world diagnostic accessibility.

Founder & Director

Mukund Ganpatrao Bhong

B.Sc. Pharmacy, D. Pharma

With over 20 years of experience in the medical and pharmaceutical industry, Mukund brings deep domain expertise and leadership to Microworld Biotech. He has been a Partner at Varad Pharmaceutical since 2016, contributing significantly to its growth and operations.

His extensive knowledge of pharmaceutical supply chains, regulatory compliance, and healthcare delivery has been instrumental in shaping Microworld Biotech's product strategy and market positioning.

Govind Chandrakant Kavtikwar
