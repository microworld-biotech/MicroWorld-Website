About Microworld Biotech

Our History

Founded in 2023, Microworld Biotech Private Limited is driven by a clear mission: to advance diagnostic solutions that are faster, more accessible, and highly reliable — for the benefit of mankind.

Established by seasoned professionals with deep expertise in pharmaceuticals and healthcare, the company is built on a practical understanding of real-world diagnostic challenges.

As a focused B2B partner, we develop and deliver advanced diagnostic solutions that combine speed, precision, and ease of use — supporting healthcare providers, laboratories, and partners across the value chain.

Guided by a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and accessibility, we aim to make reliable diagnostics available where they are needed most.

Our Technology

We utilize advanced Immunochromatography technology, designed to deliver fast and reliable diagnostic results. What makes our technology different?

High Accuracy: Precise detection with high sensitivity and specificity, reducing false results.
Rapid Results (15–20 minutes): Enables immediate clinical decisions.
No Specialized Equipment Required: Usable in clinics, nursing homes, and field conditions.
Detailed Diagnostic Insights: Semi-quantitative info such as viral load and antibody levels.
Optimized Storage & Transport: Controlled temperature handling ensures consistent quality.
Field-Ready Solutions: Ideal for remote areas, outbreaks, and resource-limited environments.
