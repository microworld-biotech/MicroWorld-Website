Dengue Combo Test Kit

NS1 Antigen & IgG/IgM Antibodies — Comprehensive. Rapid. Reliable.

Home › Products › Dengue Combo Test Kit

Clinical Significance

Dengue is a major mosquito-borne viral infection with significant global impact. Early symptoms include fever, headache, muscle pain, and skin rash, while severe cases may lead to complications such as dengue hemorrhagic fever.

This test plays a crucial role in:

  • Early detection through NS1 antigen (initial phase of infection)
  • Monitoring immune response via IgM and IgG antibodies
  • Differentiating primary and secondary infections
  • Supporting diagnosis across different stages of the disease
Rapid Diagnostic Test

Product Overview

Our Dengue Combo Test Kit is an advanced rapid diagnostic solution designed for the qualitative detection of Dengue NS1 antigen and IgG/IgM antibodies in human serum or plasma. It enables early and accurate diagnosis, supporting timely clinical decisions and effective patient management.

Utilizing advanced immunochromatography technology, this kit delivers reliable results without specialized laboratory infrastructure — ideal for hospitals, clinics, and resource-limited settings.
