FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your test kits available for individual purchase or B2B only? ▼
Our products are primarily designed for B2B customers — hospitals, diagnostic labs, clinics, nursing homes, distributors, and NGOs. For individual purchases, please contact us and we will connect you with a nearby authorised distributor.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? ▼
MOQ varies by product and geography. Please reach out to us via the contact form or WhatsApp with your requirements and we will share specific pricing and MOQ details promptly.
Do you offer product samples for evaluation? ▼
Yes, we provide product samples to verified healthcare institutions and distributors for evaluation purposes. Please use the contact form above and select "Request a Sample" as the subject to get started.
How long does delivery take across India? ▼
Delivery timelines depend on your location and order volume. Typically, orders are processed and dispatched within 2–3 business days. Standard delivery across India takes 3–7 business days. Expedited shipping can be arranged for urgent orders.
Do your test kits require cold chain storage? ▼
Our kits are designed for stability at room temperature (2–30°C) during transport and field use, making them suitable for remote settings. However, for optimal shelf life, storage within the specified temperature range on the product label is recommended.
Are your products certified and regulatory compliant? ▼
Yes, all Microworld Biotech products are developed in compliance with applicable regulatory standards. For specific certification details or documentation requests, please contact us directly.
Do you provide after-sales support and training? ▼
Absolutely. We are committed to full customer support including product training, troubleshooting assistance, and guidance on kit usage. Contact us anytime and our technical team will be happy to assist.
